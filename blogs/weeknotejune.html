cubbon reads inspired me to write this and then it went downhill


Jun 28, 2026



June has been catastrophical. My birthday month always has been. I had a message from me from last year saying "Hey... How are you? Are you ok? Cuz I'm not... I hope you're better than what I am rn... I love you. I hope you made it. I'm tryna get a job rn and can't. I feel trapped and scared... Am I going to be okay? I'm scared santrupti..."

If santrupti from last year saw me she would definitely not think I'm ok. But I think I am. A lot of things are falling apart but I'm extremely grateful for everything I do have. If I reflect on why that is, its because of the environment I've made for myself over the years. The conversations I now have with others and myself have been so nourishing that there is a sense of community and safety that I'm extremely proud of. Regardless of everything bad happening, its worth witnessing this life.

    • Never fucking mind, that was not the end. I ended up going to a meetup after writing this weeknote, it made me mad and I'm writing about it. Everybody else got to talk about things they were building and I immediately got flirted with or the conversation diverted or worse, met with disinterest. These hostile spaces make me so pissed off. We have solved this before. We know how to do these things now, you've heard enough people who are not men complain about this.
